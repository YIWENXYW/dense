322 /*********************************************************************
323  ** mutate **
324  ** ESMutate(population, param) **
325  ** **
326  ** sp_ : copy of sp **
327  ** op_ : copy of op **
328  ** update sp **
329  ** traditional technique using exponential smoothing **
330  ** sp(1->miu-1) : unchanged **
331  ** sp(miu->lambda): sp = sp_*exp(tau_*N(0,1) + tau*Nj(0,1)) **
332  ** Nj : random number generated for each j **
333  ** check sp bound **
334  ** if(sp > bound) then sp = bound **
335  ** differential variation **
336  ** op(1->miu-1) = op_ + gamma*(op_[1] - op_[i+1]) **
337  ** mutation **
338  ** op(miu->lambda): op = op_ +sp * N(0,1) **
339  ** check op bound **
340  ** if(op > ub || op < lb) then try retry times **
341  ** op = op_ + sp * N(0,1) **
342  ** if still not in bound then op = op_ **
343  ** exponential smoothing **
344  ** sp(miu->lambda): sp = sp_ + alpha * (sp - sp_) **
345  ** **
346  ** re-calculate f/g/phi **
347  *********************************************************************/
348 void ESMutate(ESPopulation *, ESParameter *);
